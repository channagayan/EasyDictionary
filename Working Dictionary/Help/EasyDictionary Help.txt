How to get the meaning for English word

Put mouse pointer on top of the word that you want to get the meaning.
Click Ctrl+Left mouse Button or Double Click (This can be configured using settings menu)
You will get the sinhala meaning near the word you clicked
Click anywhere to hide the meaning bubble. 

How to set the Key combination

Right click on the EasyDictionary icon in the task bar
Select settings menu
Select what key combination you want
Click save

EasyDictionary supports most of the web browsers, Adobe Reader, Microsoft Office, Notepad and many other text editors and word processors.
